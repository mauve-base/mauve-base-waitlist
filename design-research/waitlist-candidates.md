# Mauve Base — Waitlist/Landing Page Design Candidates

Research compiled 2026-06-17. Product context: **Mauve Base** — ground-level market
intelligence for B2B vendors selling into SMBs. Two mechanics: (1) proprietary SMB
buying-signal collection, (2) an AI warm-intro engine over a vendor↔SMB **trust graph**.
Positioning: *"the first tab open before any outreach."*

Must-haves being evaluated per candidate: 📧 email capture · 🏆 social proof · 🖼️ product preview.
⭐ = strongest conceptual fit for Mauve.

All URLs verified live at time of research.

---

## A. Minimal & clean (Linear / Vercel / Stripe school)

### ⭐ Pocus — https://pocus.com  *(closest direct waitlist precedent)*
- White + teal, friendly, minimal. **Literally runs a "Join Waitlist" CTA right now.**
- Hero: *"Make outbound feel like inbound — with AI."* CTAs "Join Waitlist" + "Take a tour."
- 🖼️ "Slide to see the magic" interactive before/after product carousel (no produced video needed).
- 🏆 Logo carousel (Webflow, Monday, Canva, Asana) + ROI band ($1.5M pipeline, 10+ hrs saved) + CRO testimonial.
- Standout: the slide-to-reveal hero — high engagement, cheap to build.

### ⭐ Default — https://default.com  *(best "show, don't tell")*
- Clean, data-forward, charcoal-on-white, subtle blue UI accents.
- Hero: *"Deploy agents that work across your go-to-market."* CTA "Request a Demo".
- 🖼️ Hero screenshot shows the product **answering a real query** ("Show me all inbound-sourced pipeline") with a results table — the demo IS the headline.
- 🏆 Grayscale logo strip (shown twice), outcome-flavored testimonials, no vanity counts (restraint as a flex).
- Standout: hero literally dramatizes "first tab before outreach."

### Attio — https://attio.com  *(craft benchmark)*
- Premium-minimal AI-native CRM. The bar for making data-heavy relationship tools feel effortless.
- Hero: "AI-native CRM / for the next generation of teams." 📧 CTA "Start for free."
- 🖼️ Crisp real-UI screenshots + a 1–3 day onboarding timeline as proof-of-value.
- 🏆 "80,000 top startups/VCs" + named logos (Coca-Cola, Modal, Lovable); *"Attio feels like magic"* testimonial.
- Standout: testimonial-as-emotion over feature lists; fast-onboarding timeline pre-empts "is this hard to set up?"

### Folk — https://folk.app  *(best email-capture pattern)*
- Clean, near-monochrome CRM with refined micro-interactions.
- Hero: *"The CRM that works for your team."* 📧 **inline email form + team-size dropdown** ("Try for free") + "Get a demo."
- 🖼️ Full-width annotated hero screenshot + 5 in-context product images.
- 🏆 "Trusted by 5000+ companies" logos + triple rating badges (Product Hunt/Chrome/G2) + testimonial carousel.
- Standout: email + team-size dropdown qualifies the lead in one field — ideal waitlist hero.

### ⭐ Warmly — https://warmly.ai  *(best trust-graph visualization)*

### Cursor — https://cursor.com  *(AI-agent messaging template)*

## Cross-cutting patterns worth stealing
1. **Quantify the graph, not just the product.** Swarm's "168 quadrillion relationships" and Crossbeam's "intelligence layer competitors can't buy" sell the *moat as a number/asset*. Put a trust-graph scale stat in the hero.
2. **Show the warm-intro email being written.** Crossbeam's live AI-chat demo (and Warmly's named engine diagram) are the most copy-worthy ideas — they *are* Mauve's core demo.
3. **Metric-bearing social proof beats logo walls.** Every strong page pairs each customer with a hard number (ROI, pipeline %, hours saved). Clay puts a metric-badge strip immediately under the hero.
4. **For a waitlist CTA:** Pocus is the only direct precedent still running "Join Waitlist." Folk's inline email + team-size dropdown is the cleanest self-serve form. Pair primary email capture with a secondary "Book a demo" + short demo video.
5. **Interactive product preview without a video:** Pocus's slide-to-reveal carousel and Resend's input-beside-output are cheap, high-engagement alternatives to a produced demo.

## Suggested top-3 shortlist for Mauve
- **Direct waitlist fit:** Pocus (waitlist motion + slide-to-reveal preview).
- **Best conceptual demo of Mauve's product:** Crossbeam (live AI drafting a warm intro) or Warmly (named trust-graph engine diagram).
- **Best premium "trust + intelligence" brand feel:** The Swarm / Attio (navy or near-black, one warm accent, oversized sans, real UI) — relationship-graph credibility done tastefully.
